Project 02

Hairdresser
Simulator

Dialogue Writing NPC Design Character Arcs
Screenshot

In Hairdresser Simulator, the player meets 27 unique NPCs, each of whom returns to the salon three times over the course of their personal arc. I designed every one of these characters with a focus on emotional nuance and storytelling through small, intimate moments.

With each visit, players uncover an event or shift in the NPC's life that brought them back — whether for comfort, reinvention, or a bit of peace. These glimpses into the characters' lives allow players to gradually piece together who they are, creating the quiet, satisfying fantasy of a hairdresser who becomes a trusted confidante.

A branching quest design sample demonstrating character-driven investigation, meaningful player choice, and multi-path resolution with lasting consequences.

Sapsorrow

Quest Design Document — Sample

Objective

Investigate the aberrations that plague the village of Mournstead.

Key Characters & Locations

Mournstead

Location

A small village with a big problem; an isolated frontier settlement facing dying crops and mysterious attacks on nearby roads.

The Tree of Life

Key Object

A gnarled ancient tree, object of cult worship. Each autumn equinox it produces red healing apples — rumored to restore missing limbs.

Aria

Potential Companion

Local midwife and druidess: quiet, knowledgeable, too curious for her own good. Wants to preserve traditions and the tree. Has a unique connection to nature.

Tyrell

Sheriff

Young, stern, suspicious of superstition. Wants to help Mournstead establish better trade routes but hasn't yet earned his stripes.

Quest Steps

  1. 01

    Defend yourself. The PC is ambushed by sentient vine-like creatures, unlike anything they've encountered before.

  2. 02

    Speak with locals. Sympathetic but reluctant, they direct the PC to Tyrell or Aria depending on personal views — they seem aware of the problem.

  3. 03

    Choose your contact. Tyrell hires the PC to investigate the tree. Aria shows her clinic and the miracles the Tree has performed — and offers side-quests. A PC following Tyrell can still help Aria.

  4. 04

    Investigate the Tree. Using Nature, Investigation, History, or Religion skills — or the local library — the PC learns the Tree draws power from murdered travelers. Additional lore scales with rolls and knowledge sources.

  5. 05

    Pick a course of action. Persuade Aria the Tree does more harm than good. Lie to Tyrell for personal gain — fruit, branches, crafting items. Or pursue another path entirely.

  6. 06

    Face the Tree Spirit. Attacks if summoned without proper ritual, or if the PC takes a branch. If Aria is present, she barely survives — the Tree trusts her for her longtime service.

  7. 07

    Make your final choice. If Aria was harmed, she becomes dependent on the Tree. Sacrifice herself for the town? Join the party searching for another solution — losing Tyrell's favor? Or destroy the Tree, killing Aria, but gaining a prosperous ally town with lower trade prices and mercs for hire.
